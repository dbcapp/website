About us

- Learn more about our organization

DiBoaCara is an open-source project that enables users to easily search, donnate and bound to non profit organizations

Our History

This project/platform was created at koding global hackathon as a open source project. We have a simple goal: to help NGOs get funded and to create a strong bond between the NGO and its donnator.

What we do

We empower people bound and donate to a cause they support (NGO), making it easier for NGOs to be found and receive donation money for their cause.

Why donate with us

We believe in transparency above averything else. Our platform is fully open sourced within the GPL license, meaning that every product and platform change needs to be public available (by us or other team).
